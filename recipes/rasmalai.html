Rasmalai

Description

This is a homemade recipe for a famous Indian sweet dessert

Enjoy the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 litre full cream milk
  • ½ litre fat free milk
  • 250g sugar
  • Saffrons
  • Elaichi
  • Paneer
  • Pista

Steps

  1. Warm 2 litre full cream milk to the point that it becomes half
  2. After it becomes half, add 5 spoons sugar and add 10-12 Saffrons
  3. Keep it cool in fridge for 5-6 hours
  4. Make Paneer ½ fat free milk
  5. 1 cube of Amul Paneer packet
  6. Mix those two paneer with reverse mode
  7. Batt Paneer on the plate
  8. Mix 1½ teaspoon cornflour
  9. Mix proper and prepare a proper dough alteast for 10 minutes
  10. Divide it into equal parts
  11. Add 1 small boul sugar and 5 warm water in Pan
  12. Add Paneer Pedas in the pan
  13. Without covering cook it for 5 minutes
  14. Lightly turn and cook for another 5 minutes
  15. Again turn and now cover with lid and cook for 5 minutes
  16. Again turn and with covered lid and cook for 5 minutes
  17. If they are doubled the size, stop the gas
  18. Keep it till cool and transfer it into milk
  19. Serve it with Pista
