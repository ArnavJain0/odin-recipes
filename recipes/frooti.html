Frooti

Description

This is a homemade recipe for a fresh mango juice

You can enjoy this recipe in hot summers

Ingredients

  • 2 riped mangoes
  • 1 raw mango
  • 1 glass sugar
  • 6 glass water
  • pinch of food(based on your choice)

Steps

  1. Wash mangoes and dry them
  2. Peel and cut all mangoes
  3. Put them in a cooker
  4. Add 1 glass sugar
  5. Add 6 glass water
  6. Add color
  7. 5-6 whistle on high flame
  8. Cool it and then gr:ind it in mixer
  9. Strain the juice and fill it up in a bottle
  10. Keep in fridge to cool
  11. Enjoy the drink
